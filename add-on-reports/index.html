report-all

All Extensions compatible with TB60 or newer.

,

report-all-with-parsing-error

Extensions whose XPI files could not be parsed properly and are excluded from analysis.

,

report-all-recent-activity

Extensions updated within the last 2 weeks.

,

report-all-wrong-order

Extension with wrong upper limit setting in older versions, which will lead to the wrong version reported compatible by ATN.

,

report-all-experiments-atn-value-reduced-below-xpi

Extensions whose max version has been reduced in ATN below the XPI value (which is ignored during install and app upgrade).

,

report-all-latest-current-mismatch

Extensions, where the latest upload is for an older release, which will fail to install in current ESR (current = defined current in ATN) from within the add-on manager.

,

report-all-without-upper-limit

Extensions without upper limit in ATN (excluding Experiments). This should list only pure MailExtension.

,

report-atn-tb60

Extensions compatible with Thunderbird 60 as seen by ATN.

,

report-atn-tb68

Extensions compatible with Thunderbird 68 as seen by ATN.

,

report-atn-tb78

Extensions compatible with Thunderbird 78 as seen by ATN.

,

report-atn-tb91

Extensions compatible with Thunderbird 91 as seen by ATN.

,

report-false-positives-tb68

Extensions claiming to be compatible with Thunderbird 68, but are legacy extensions and therefore unsupported.

,

report-false-positives-tb78

Extensions claiming to be compatible with Thunderbird 78, but are legacy extensions or legacy WebExtensions and therefore unsupported.

,

report-lost-tb60-to-tb68

Extensions which have been lost from TB60 to TB68.

,

report-lost-tb68-to-tb78

Extensions which have been lost from TB68 to TB78.

,

report-lost-tb78-to-tb91

Extensions which have been lost from TB78 to TB91.

,

report-tb91-pure-mx-incompatible

[TB91] Pure MailExtensions, marked incompatible with TB91, which they probably are not.

,

report-tb91-experiments-without-upper-limit

[TB91] Experiments without upper limit in ATN, which might not be compatible with TB91 (excluding reported positives).
